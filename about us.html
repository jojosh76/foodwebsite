About Us
Our project is a digital canteen platform designed for ICT University students. With this system, students will be able to browse meals, place food orders online, make secure payments, and simply collect their plates upon arrival at the canteen. Our goal is to reduce waiting time, improve convenience, and make the entire canteen experience faster and more efficient.

This project was developed collaboratively by our group of six motivated students.
