Our Project

Our project is a digital canteen platform designed for ICT University students. The system allows students to browse available meals, place food orders online, make secure payments, and simply collect their meals upon arrival at the canteen.

The main goal of this platform is to reduce waiting time, improve convenience, and make the overall canteen experience faster and more efficient for students.

This project was developed collaboratively by a group of five motivated students, each contributing specific skills and responsibilities to ensure the success of the system.
